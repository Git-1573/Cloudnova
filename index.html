Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services? Check out the most common questions we receive. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us!

1. What is SwiftDesign?

SwiftDesign is a creative web design agency that offers custom website development, pre-designed templates, WordPress development, and hosting solutions to help businesses establish a powerful online presence.

2. Can I customize the SwiftDesign templates?

Yes! Our SwiftDesign templates are fully customizable. You can adjust colors, fonts, layout, and other elements to fit your brand’s style and needs.

3. What is included in the Custom Website plan?

The Custom Website plan includes a tailored design, up to 10 pages, free domain for 2 years, business email setup, form integrations, daily backups, and one year of free maintenance. Hosting and SSL certificates are also included.

4. Is WordPress included in the WordPress Website plan?

Yes, the WordPress Website plan includes the setup and configuration of WordPress, along with a custom design, form integrations, free domain for 2 years, and business email setup.

5. Do I need to purchase hosting separately?

No, we offer reliable hosting solutions as part of our Custom and WordPress Website plans. It includes daily backups, free SSL certificates, and 24/7 customer support.

6. What kind of support do you offer after the website is launched?

We offer 1 year of free maintenance for both custom and WordPress websites. This includes updates, bug fixes, and basic support. After the first year, we offer continued support at an additional cost.

7. Do you provide SEO services?

Yes! We offer SEO services as an add-on to improve your website's visibility on search engines and drive organic traffic. Let us help you optimize your site and improve your search ranking.

8. Can I upgrade my website plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your website plan at any time. Whether you want to add e-commerce features, extra pages, or additional services, we can accommodate your needs as your business grows.
